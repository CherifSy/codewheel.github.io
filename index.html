Purpose & Contact

Codewheel is the design and development practice of Sam Margalit

I like making systems.

Sometimes that means I get to develop a design language that can be used to thoughtfully and clearly communicate information and personality. Other times it lets me solve development problems like how to build maintainable user interfaces.

I’m comfortable working with teams, existing codebases and even learning new programming languages. I’m at my best when on the frontend using libraries like React.js or designing user experiences for products.

These days I’m mostly interested in product design and functional programming. If you think your project might be a good fit for me please get in touch.
